Manual Hover Test Instructions

  1. Open http://localhost:3001/test-game
  2. Click on the canvas twice at the same position to stack 2 blocks
  3. Move your mouse over the stacked blocks - you should see a yellow hover outline at the top of the stack
  4. Move your mouse over an empty area - you should see a yellow hover outline at ground level
  5. The hover outline should be clearly visible and yellow instead of the old white

Expected Behavior:

  • ✅ Yellow hover outline appears when hovering over tiles
  • ✅ Outline appears elevated when hovering over stacked blocks
  • ✅ Outline appears at ground level when hovering over empty tiles
  • ✅ Outline follows mouse movement smoothly

If it's not working:

  • ❌ No hover outline appears - hover events not working
  • ❌ Outline is white instead of yellow - color change didn't apply
  • ❌ Outline always at ground level - height calculation not working
